Forms of Sound

A project done while at the Royal College of Art, Based in Hackney Wick a small inner-city borough in northeast London. Hackney Wick is one of the boroughs at the edge of the Olympic Park, being one of the areas left in-tact, looking at the area in its current condition, the changes to come from the Olympics, and whether or not the area will become derelict like the Athens Olympic Park or a hub for social and economic growth. Hackney Wick is surrounded by Highways, Trains (Both Commuter and Industrial), and buses. The area has a ghost town quality, but the sound pollution in Wick is loud at all times of the day. The video demonstrates that sound based on a particle reaction to that sound to show its diversity with each element and how they react together.
